## Information

The Vanguard Real Estate Index Fund ETF (VNQ) is an exchange-traded fund that tracks the performance of an index composed of U.S. real estate investment trusts (REITs) and other companies involved in the real estate sector. 
The fund aims to provide investors with exposure to the U.S. real estate market and its potential income and diversification benefits.
The tracked index is the MSCI US IMI/Real Estate 25-50 Index, which includes about 169 holdings across various subsectors such as residential, office, industrial, retail, and specialized REITs.
