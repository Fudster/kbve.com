## Information

The iShares U.S. Real Estate ETF (IYR) is an exchange-traded fund that tracks the performance of an index composed of U.S. equities in the real estate sector.
The index is the Dow Jones U.S. Real Estate Index, which includes 96 holdings across various subsectors such as specialized, residential, office, industrial, and retail REITs.
