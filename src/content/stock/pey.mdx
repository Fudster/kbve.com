## Information

The Invesco High Yield Equity Dividend Achievers ETF (PEY) is an exchange-traded fund that tracks the performance of an index composed of U.S. companies that have increased their annual dividend for at least 10 consecutive years.
The index is the NASDAQ US Dividend Achievers 50 Index, which includes 50 holdings across various sectors such as utilities, consumer staples, financials, and industrials.
